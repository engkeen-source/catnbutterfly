How you use the gift of life depends on how much energy you have. When you are energised, you would feel a general lightness and playfulness, have a sense of control yet fully accepting, and act with more compassion and awareness — the day would just seem better.

David Hawkins in his monumental work *"The Map of Consciousness Explained"* shows that the energy level reflected in our emotional state attributes a certain life-view and God-view, thus directly affecting how we live. Here's the most familiar 6 states (out of the 17 states listed), from low to high energy states, that works to shift our reality:

1. If our energy state settles at **shame** and **humiliation**, we believe "God despises me", thus life becomes miserable — *I am not worthy.*
2. If our energy state settles at **fear** and **anxiety**, we believe "God is punitive", thus life becomes frightening — *I must withdraw to protect myself.*
3. If our energy state settles at **anger** and **hate**, we believe "God is vengeful", thus life becomes antagonistic — *I must fight against others.*
4. If our energy state settles at **courage** and **control**, we believe "God is enabling", thus life becomes empowered — *I can choose.*
5. If our energy state settles at **reason** and **understanding**, we believe "God is wise", thus life becomes meaningful — *I matter.*
6. If our energy state settles at **love** and **acceptance**, we believe "God is merciful", thus life becomes joyful — *I am complete.*

To function at a higher energy state, thus a more blissful life, requires conscious management of our energy level at every moment. It is important to keep track of how your energy level fluctuates: what charges you and what drains you, so you can design a routine that replenish your energy level consistently.
